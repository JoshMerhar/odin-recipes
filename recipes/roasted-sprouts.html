Roasted Brussels Sprouts

They taste better than they smell. But I've grown to like their funk...

These roasted brussels sprouts are super easy to prepare and don't require many ingredients. Even an abject knucklehead like me can consistently make this recipe well. All it really takes is some olive oil, salt, pepper, balsamic vinegar, a drizzle of honey, and a total of 30-35 minutes. I make them at least once a week. It's probably the single healthiest thing I routinely consume.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of brussels sprouts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Cover a large baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  3. Cut brussels sprouts into halves, placing flat side down on the baking sheet.
  4. Coat with 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  5. Sprinkle liberally with salt and pepper.
  6. Stir sprouts around baking sheet, ensuring they return to flat side down.
  7. Place into oven, bake for 11 minutes.
  8. Stir sprouts around baking sheet, again ensuring they return to flat side down.
  9. Place into oven again for another 11 minutes.
  10. When finished baking, remove sprouts from oven.
  11. Coat sprouts in remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Splash with 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar.
  12. Drizzle with honey and stir to coat evenly.
  13. Skull those sprouts!
