Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Description

These roasted brussels sprouts are super easy to prepare and don't require many ingredients. Even an abject knucklehead like me can consistently make this recipe well. All it really takes is some olive oil, salt, pepper, balsamic vinegar, a drizzle of honey, and a total of 30-35 minutes.

Get your oven rippin' at 450 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil. Halve the sprouts. Lay the sprouts flat side down and distribute them evenly across the baking sheet. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon of oilive oil. Sprinkle with salt and ground pepper. Push the sprouts around the baking sheet, ensuring they all get a coating of oil, salt, and pepper. Make sure to return all sprouts to flat side down before putting them in the oven.Place in the oven for 11 minutes.

After 11 minutes, pull the sprouts from the oven. Scooch them around the baking sheet to prevent them from burning. Again, make sure they all return to flat side down before placing them back into the oven. Place in the oven for another 11 minutes. When finished baking, remove from oven. Drizzle with a bit more olive oil, no more than 1 tablespoon. Splash with 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar, or more if you'd like. Drizzle with honey. Serve immediately.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of brussels sprouts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F
  2. Coat a large baking sheet with aluminum foil
  3. Cut brussels sprouts into halves, placing flat side down on the baking sheet
  4. Coat with 1 tablespoon olive oil
  5. Sprinkle liberally with salt and pepper
  6. Stir sprouts around baking sheet, ensuring they return to flat side down
  7. Place into oven, bake for 11 minutes
  8. Stir sprouts around baking sheet, again ensuring they return to flat side down
  9. Place into oven again for another 11 minutes
  10. When finished baking, remove sprouts from oven
  11. Coat sprouts in remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Splash with 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar
  12. Drizzle with honey and stir to coat evenly
  13. Skull those sprouts!
