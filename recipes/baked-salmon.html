Basic Baked Salmon

Always be on the lookout for stray bones.

Cooking salmon in the oven is one of the easiest ways to ingest some delicious and nutricious food. Just slap a little bit of olive oil on a salmon filet, sprinkle with salt, pepper, and a sparse dusting of garlic powder, and you'll be most of the way there. Add a pat of butter on top before placing into the oven if you feel real adventurous.

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon filet per person
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Cover a large baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  3. Dab salmon dry with paper towel.
  4. Brush on olive oil to coat fish evenly.
  5. Add salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  6. Add pat of butter (optional).
  7. Place into oven, bake for 16-18 minutes.
    • Thicker cuts of fish may take longer.
  8. Fish should flake apart easily when cooked.
  9. Use a spatula to remove fish from skin.
  10. Skull that salmon!
