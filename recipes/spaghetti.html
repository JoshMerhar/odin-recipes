Spaghetti and Meatballs

It tastes better when everything is from Whole Foods.

Sometimes, I'm just too beat to cook something serious or it's too late to order food. It's times like those where trusty Spaghetti and Meatballs save the day. All you need is a box of dried spaghetti, a bag of frozen meatballs, and a jar of pasta sauce. It doesn't get much more basic and pathetic than that.

Ingredients

  • 1 box dried spaghetti
  • 1 jar pasta sauce of your choice
  • Bag of frozen meatballs
  • Grated parmesan
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot roughly 2/3 with water and bring to boil.
    • Add a liberal amount of salt to the water.
  2. Empty jar of pasta sauce into a medium sauce pan over low heat.
  3. As sauce warms, add frozen meatballs to sauce.
  4. Simmer sauce and meatballs for 20 minutes or until meatballs are tender.
  5. Add spaghetti to boiling salted water in large pot.
  6. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
    • Read package for time instructions.
  7. Drain spaghetti in a colander.
  8. Return spaghetti to the pot it was cooked in.
  9. Pour meatballs and sauce over spaghetti.
  10. Serve spaghetti and meatballs onto a plate and top with a concerning amount of parmesan.
  11. Skull that 'sghetti!
